Level Inf — Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2025

Your Data Stays on Your Device

Level Inf stores all your habit data — completions, streaks, and settings — entirely on your device using a local database (SQLite). We do not collect, transmit, or store your personal habit data on any server.

What We Store Locally

  • Habit names, emojis, and completion history
  • Your name and avatar (entered during onboarding)
  • App settings (theme, notification preferences)
  • Subscription status (via Google Play Billing)

None of this information leaves your device.

Subscriptions & Payments

Premium subscriptions are processed entirely by Google Play Billing. Level Inf does not handle or store any payment information. Subscription management (cancel, upgrade, restore) is handled through your Google Play account. We use RevenueCat to verify subscription status — this sends an anonymous device identifier to RevenueCat servers to validate your purchase receipt.

Notifications

Level Inf uses local notifications (scheduled on your device) for habit reminders. No notification data is sent to external servers.

Analytics & Crash Reporting

We may collect anonymous crash reports to improve app stability. No personally identifiable information is included. You can opt out in Settings.

Children's Privacy

Level Inf is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy. Changes will be reflected in the "Last updated" date above. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance.
